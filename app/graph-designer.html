What we’re looking for

Responsibilities

Communication with the English-speaking client with the purpose to find out requirements for design of future projects, considering that the client does not always know what he wants.

Qualifications

  • Excellent understanding of the UI creation principles in mobile and web solutions;
  • UI/UX Experience;
  • Ability to ask the correct questions, finding out requirements to the system;
  • Desire to penetrate an essence into a project and get to the bottom of trifles;
  • Excellent English for daily communications with American customers;
  • Independent, initiative and communicative.
